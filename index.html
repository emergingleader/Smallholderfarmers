What the longitudinal data tells us

Income gains compound significantly over time — Nduti income grew a further 79% between follow-up and endline. Savings amounts also keep increasing (Momul: KES 677 → 800 → 1,500). This confirms the programme creates durable change, not just short-term shifts. Notably, Momul's ripple effect reached ×6 at endline — above the ×5 baseline — suggesting community diffusion is still expanding.
